Popualar Japanese Foods

Japan has many types of delicious food. You can find great and unique cuisine from traditional one, many forein foods to evolving new styles.


  • Sushi
  • Noodles
  • Curry Rice
  • Fries
  • Rice Bowls
  • Sweets

Sushi

Sushi might be one of the most popular Japanese food in the world.


Noodles

Japanese food has some types of noodles. Ramen is lately getting popular worldwide. Some famouse ramen restaurants often get a long line. Udon is white noodle which is made from wheat flour. Soba is gray noodle which is made from buckwheat flour.

Curry Rice

Curry was brought from India by Britain. As Japanese serve curry on rice, it is called "Curry Rice" in Japanese. Curry rice is one of the most popular dish in Japan. Pepole like curry-flavored foods such as
curry in bread, curry-flavored fried rice, curry-flavored udon and so on.

Fries

Japanese cuisine have some fried dishes such as fried chicken, tempra, fried pork etc. Fried chicken is marinated with soy sauce which makes its taste rich. Tempura is also one of the famous Japanese dishes overseas. Seafoods and vegetables are used. Fried pork is good when people want to get more energy.

Rice Bowls

Rice Bowls are quick meals with variety of tastes. Beef bowls which consist of thin-cut beef saute on the rice are popular. We can put tempura, or put raw fish and so on.

Sweets

There are tons of many great sweets in Japan. We can still find traditional Japanese sweets, and sweets from western countries such as cookies, cakes etc. are also common. These have been evolved with Japanese-taste.
